🌿 Plant Care Guide

📝 Why Care for Plants?

Indoor plants purify the air, add beauty to your home, and reduce stress. Caring for them not only enhances your environment but improves your well-being.

🌱 Easy-to-Care Plants

Snake Plant

Hardy and perfect for beginners. Needs little water and indirect sunlight.

Peace Lily

Beautiful white flowers. Likes humidity and low to medium light.

ZZ Plant

Glossy leaves, drought-tolerant. Thrives in almost any light condition.

Spider Plant

Air-purifying and fast-growing. Enjoys bright, indirect sunlight.

Aloe Vera

Medicinal plant, stores water in its leaves. Needs bright light and minimal watering.

Pothos

Great hanging plant, grows quickly and needs minimal care. Can survive low light.

✅ Care Tips

✔ Water when top soil is dry
✔ Use pots with drainage holes
✔ Wipe leaves occasionally to keep them clean
✔ Avoid overwatering – it's the #1 mistake!
